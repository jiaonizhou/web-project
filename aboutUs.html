What we do

wedding, corporate, and social events industry. Launched in 2016, PartyJoy is the largest
and most trusted online marketplace connecting merchants with engaged couples and
party planners. Moreover, PartyJoy's SaaS business platform powers the front and
back-office for tens of thousands of event merchants, from venues and photographers
to caterers and entertainers.
